⚠ Servo power: Drive the PCA9685's V+ from a separate 5–6 V supply (e.g. UBEC, bench PSU, or fresh 4× AA pack), with its GND tied to the Pi's GND. Do not power servos from the Pi's 5 V rail — typical hobby-servo stall current (~1 A each, ×5) will brown out the Pi and risk corrupting the SD card.

Pin assignments mirror esp32_emg_streamer.ino (the active dual-channel UDP firmware). Edit it (or this diagram) in lockstep if you reassign pins. Sampling: 1 kHz × 2 ch, packet size 48 bytes.
